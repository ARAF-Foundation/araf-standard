Implementation Guidance for Organisations

ARAF is a governance architecture standard. The methodology pages define what the standard assesses and how it scores. This section answers the practical implementation questions teams ask before engagement:

- which systems to assess
- when to conduct an assessment
- how long assessments typically take
- what internal preparation should be completed
- who should lead the process

Organisations at any stage can engage with ARAF. The framework scales from Seed-stage organisations with a single autonomous system to enterprises operating multi-system portfolios with formal governance committees. The accountability standard remains the same: consequential decisions must be reconstructable from contemporaneous governance records.
