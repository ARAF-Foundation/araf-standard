---
title: Evidence Checklist
description: Pilot evidence checklist for building a contemporaneous governance record aligned with ARAF dimensional and certification requirements.
---

This checklist helps pilot teams produce evidence that can support dimensional scoring, GBI interpretation, and certification pathway planning.

## 1. Pilot Definition

- [ ] Pilot name, owner, and accountable executive recorded
- [ ] System boundary and authorised autonomous actions defined
- [ ] Commitment thresholds and stop conditions documented
- [ ] In-scope jurisdictions and legal context identified

## 2. Decision Supply Chain Mapping

- [ ] End-to-end chain mapped from data input to execution outcome
- [ ] Internal and external parties identified by governance role
- [ ] Accountability assignments recorded for each chain link
- [ ] Boundary points for contract and liability obligations identified

## 3. Dimensional Evidence Minimums

### Dimension 1 (Autonomy Gradient)

- [ ] Decision authority definitions
- [ ] Scope boundary controls
- [ ] Human oversight trigger rules

### Dimension 2 (Data Sensitivity Exposure)

- [ ] Data class inventory
- [ ] Access and handling controls
- [ ] Training or retrieval provenance records where relevant

### Dimension 3 (Contract Infrastructure)

- [ ] Executed agreements for participating providers
- [ ] Governance and audit clauses
- [ ] Responsibility allocation at execution boundaries

### Dimension 4 (Liability Architecture)

- [ ] Liability allocation map
- [ ] Cap and carve-out terms
- [ ] Insurance position and coverage notes

### Dimension 5 (Commercial Leverage)

- [ ] Dependency concentration analysis
- [ ] Remediation leverage points
- [ ] Escalation pathway where supplier non-compliance occurs

### Dimension 6 (Adaptive Stability)

- [ ] Change-control workflow for model or policy updates
- [ ] Monitoring and alerting records
- [ ] Periodic governance review cadence

## 4. Evidence Quality Tiering

- [ ] Tier 1 evidence identified where governance systems produce tamper-evident records
- [ ] Tier 2 evidence identified where contemporaneous documentation supports governance decisions
- [ ] Tier 3 evidence flagged where records are reconstructed and confidence is lower
- [ ] Evidence uplift actions assigned to reduce Tier 3 dependency

## 5. Pilot Governance Outputs

- [ ] Pilot dimensional profile draft
- [ ] Preliminary GBI interpretation and multiplier check
- [ ] Remediation roadmap with owners and due dates
- [ ] Board or governance committee briefing pack

## 6. Readiness Review

- [ ] Evidence pack is complete and export-ready
- [ ] Ownership for ongoing evidence maintenance assigned
- [ ] Residual governance risks accepted or remediated
- [ ] Pilot decision recorded: proceed, proceed with conditions, or pause
