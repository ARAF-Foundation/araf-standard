# Institutions

ARAF provides a structured governance signal for institutions seeking comparable, defensible, and usable governance signals for autonomous systems.

## Opening

Institutions require governance signals that are:

* comparable
* defensible
* usable without reconstructing methodology

ARAF provides that signal.

## Regulatory Alignment

The ARAF framework aligns with the governance and accountability requirements reflected in:

* EU AI Act
* NIST AI Risk Management Framework
* ISO/IEC 42001
* APRA CPS 230
* Corporations Act (duty of care)

The framework provides a structured method for mapping organisational governance to these requirements in a comparable format.

---

## Institutional Advisory (in formation)

ARAF is establishing an institutional advisory group to support the ongoing development of the standard.

The advisory group will include representatives from:

* insurance
* investment
* regulatory
* enterprise governance

Its role is to:

* provide feedback on institutional usability
* inform methodology evolution
* ensure alignment with real-world decision processes
