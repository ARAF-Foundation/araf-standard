The ARAF Certificate Directory provides public verification of certification status under the ARAF scheme-governance framework. Certification must be verified here—this is the authoritative record.

---

## Status Definitions

- **Active:** Certification is valid and may be referenced externally.
- **Suspended:** Certification is temporarily not valid; marks must not be used.
- **Withdrawn:** Certification has been removed due to breach or non-compliance; marks must not be used.
- **Lapsed:** Certification has expired; marks must not be used.

Certification marks must not be used when status is not Active.

---

## Scope Clarity

Certification applies only to the defined scope. It must not be represented as applying beyond that scope.

---

## Directory Updates

The directory is updated upon issuance, suspension, withdrawal, or expiry of certification. Status changes are reflected without delay following determination under the scheme-governance framework.

---

## Relationship to Certification

Certification is governed under the ARAF scheme-governance framework. The directory reflects the current state of all certifications and is not a marketing list.
