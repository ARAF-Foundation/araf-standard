This guidance note provides assessor methodology for cross-border deployment assessment in Decision Supply Chain contexts.

## Scope

GN-002 covers:

- jurisdictional footprint mapping procedures
- cross-border liability architecture requirements
- regulatory exposure mapping methodology
- tax and commercial structuring considerations
- brand and reputational architecture assessment

## Relationship to Core Standard Documents

GN-002 is a supplementary assessor reference. It supports, but does not replace, normative requirements in:

- [Decision Supply Chain](/decision-supply-chain/)
- [Six ARAF Dimensions](/six-dimensions/)
- [Certification Framework](/certification/certification-framework/)
- [Governance Coherence Addendum](/certification/governance-coherence-addendum/)

## Publication Status

GN-002 content is to be drafted from ADA v1.1 Domain V source material. This page establishes the published reference location and scope pending full guidance publication.
