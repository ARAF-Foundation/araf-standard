# GN-002: Cross-Border Deployment Assessment

*Assessor Guidance Note GN-002*

This guidance note provides assessor methodology for cross-border deployment assessment in Decision Supply Chain contexts. It extends GN-001 and must be applied in conjunction with it, not as a standalone document.

### Scope

GN-002 covers:

- jurisdictional footprint mapping procedures
- dimensional scoring adjustments for cross-border deployments
- regulatory exposure mapping methodology
- jurisdictional liability architecture considerations
- tax and commercial structuring implications
- infrastructure dependency exposure
- brand and reputational architecture assessment

### Relationship to Core Standard Documents

GN-002 is a supplementary assessor reference. It supports, but does not replace, normative requirements in:

- [Decision Supply Chain](/standard/decision-supply-chain/)
- [Six ARAF Dimensions](/standard/six-dimensions/)
- [Certification Framework](/certification/certification-framework/)
- [Governance Coherence Addendum](/certification/governance-coherence-addendum/)

GN-002 extends GN-001 Sections 2, 3, 5, and 7. All other GN-001 procedures apply without modification to cross-border assessments.
