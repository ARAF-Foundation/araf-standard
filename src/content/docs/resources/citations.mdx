Use these citation formats for institutional reports, policy references, and assessment records.

## Core Standard Citation

Agentic Risk Architecture Framework (ARAF), Version 3.0. Maintained by ARAF Standards Authority Ltd (a company limited by guarantee), 2026

## Section Citation Format

"[Section Title]," in Agentic Risk Architecture Framework (ARAF), Version [x.x]. Maintained by ARAF Standards Authority Ltd (a company limited by guarantee), [year]

## Version Discipline

Always include the explicit ARAF version and publication year in citations.
