Welcome to the ARAF Getting Started guide. This section provides practical steps and resources to help your organisation prepare for an autonomous system governance assessment.

## Preparation Steps

- Map your Decision Supply Chain
- Assemble governance documentation
- Assign accountability across the four-link chain
- Complete dimensional pre-assessment
- Begin building evidence infrastructure to the EIS-01 standard
