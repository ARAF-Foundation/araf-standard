## What Shortens Duration

- Governance records already organised by evidence category
- Decision Supply Chain map already maintained
- Dimensional pre-assessment completed before assessor engagement
- Tier 1 evidence infrastructure already in place

## What Lengthens Duration

- Documentation assembled from scratch
- Unmapped multi-provider or multi-jurisdiction supply chain
- Scope ambiguity across systems
- Legacy contracts without autonomous decision governance terms
- Parallel multi-system assessment start without phased sequencing
