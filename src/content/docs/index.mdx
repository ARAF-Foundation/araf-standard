## Three Core Elements

- **Decision Supply Chain** - the sequence of systems, human actors, and infrastructure through which a consequential decision is produced.
- **Six Governance Dimensions** - the structured methodology used to assess the governance posture of that decision infrastructure.
- **Governance Benchmark Index** - the classification signal that translates governance posture into a comparable institutional metric.
